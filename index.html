About Me

I am a technical writer and content developer with extensive experience documenting software, hardware, and firmware products in the computer and data-storage industry. I am currently studying web development at UNH so I expand my career beyond content creation and improve the way that "documentation" is accessed, delivered, and used by incorporating intelligent design with video support that provides a comprehensive solution.

Beyond my technical career, I am also an adjunct physics professor for several colleges in the Manchester, NH area. I understand that delivering a 3-hr physics lecture after an 8-hr work day may not be everyone's definition of relaxing, but I thoroughly enjoy the interaction with the students and the challenges that the job presents.
